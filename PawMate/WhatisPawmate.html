Project Overview

Project Inspiration

Born from the emotional pain point of modern urban pet owners who "are too busy to spend time with their pets," we aim to eliminate this guilt and help every responsible pet owner find reliable temporary caregivers who treat pets like family when they're busy.

Macro Benchmarking

Like "Uber for pet care," we've adopted a market-proven two-sided platform model that connects pet owners with dog walkers/caregivers, building trust through platform-verified identities, pet care knowledge, and user reviews.

Core Value

More than just a service platform, we're committed to building a sustainable ecosystem that solves social problems, enhances community trust, empowers student growth, and brings happier, healthier lives to pets.

Problem Statement

The current pet service market has significant gaps, creating a vicious cycle that affects pet owners, pets, and community harmony.

1

Insufficient and Rigid Service Supply

Traditional pet stores have fixed service hours and cannot meet sudden, flexible care needs; private nannies are expensive and difficult to verify for reliability.

2

Lack of Trust System

Pet owners find it hard to trust strangers with their pets, whom they consider family. Online platforms have disorganized information and lack unified verification and trust背书.

3

Escalating Community Conflicts

Pets lacking exercise and companionship due to busy owners may develop behavioral issues (such as barking, destruction), leading to neighborhood disputes and undermining community harmony.

Solution

For urban pet owners trapped in fragmented and unreliable pet services, we provide a comprehensive pet care platform that integrates trustworthy services, standardized processes, and community connections.

    Service Process

    1

    Login & Role Selection

    Quick login via WeChat authorization and select your identity - "I'm a Pet Owner" or "I'm a Caregiver". This precisely positions user needs and initiates the corresponding service process.

    One-click WeChat login, no registration required

    Dual-role design to meet different user needs

    Personalized user experience with precise service matching

    2

    Publish Request

    Pet owners fill out a basic service request form with core information including: service date, time, location, and pet basic information. The simple and clear form design makes the booking process easy and fast.

    Intuitive date and time picker

    Map positioning for precise service location selection

    One-click submission for quick booking completion

    3

    Matching & Confirmation

    The platform intelligently matches and displays qualified caregivers based on requirements. Pet owners can view caregiver profiles, add more detailed pet information and special requirements, and finally confirm the order.

    Intelligent matching algorithm for precise recommendations

    Transparent caregiver profile display

    Detailed pet information supplement

    4

    Execution & Tracking

    During the service, owners can view the real-time service status. After completion, the system requires caregivers to upload service photos as completion certificates, providing owners with intuitive "peace of mind feedback," and finally complete online payment.

    Real-time tracking to stay updated on service status

    Photo feedback to visually demonstrate service results

    Multiple payment methods, safe and convenient

    Market Analysis

    Three major trends together create the perfect timing for PawMate's entry, with market demand and supply conditions now mature.

    Social Attitude Shift

    The "pet humanization" trend is becoming increasingly apparent, with owners willing to pay for their pets' "happiness" and their own "peace of mind." Meanwhile, the blurring boundaries between work and life have made flexible care services a necessity.

    Market Pain Points Highlighted

    The current lack of service supply and trust deficit have reached a critical point, with the market calling for a standardized, platform-based solution to provide reliable service options for pet owners.

    Key Elements Mature

    College students form a large and high-quality potential service provider group (with time, love, and part-time needs), while the asset-light platform model allows us to launch quickly and scale efficiently.
